EVIDA

Evida is a South African DJ duo consisting of electronic music artists Liam and Richard Jones. They released their first original single, "You Gotta” on November 25, 2016. The single charted on several national radio stations giving them their first solo number one hit as well as earning them a South African Dance Music Award nomination.

The duo are no strangers to the South African airwaves. Their remix of Majozi and Tresor’s “The Woods” was notably playlisted on 94.7, 5FM, OFM and Jacaranda and was number 1 on the 5fm, OFM and Jacaranda charts. With help from the Toon53 made music video the song skyrocketed to success earning them their first SAMA nomination and has over a million streams on Spotify and over 2 million streams in total.

Their second single "1, 2, 3" with Carla Franco was signed to UMG and playlisted on 5FM as well as other local stations, named Hottest Track in South Africa by 5FM, and hit the Top of the Dance and mainstream charts.

The brothers have also produced for local super star Lady Zamar on her album Monarch as well as international artists, Dominic Neill, Wissam Hilal , Anthony Touma, Boybreed and now on with their latest release, Timmy Knight.

The latest single ‘Back To You’ showcases Timmy Knight’s astonishing song writing over an infectious uplifting beat produced by Evida. Keeping with the song’s moving lyrical content, Liam Jones shares his own vocal performance which flows harmoniously into Timmy’s and is an introduction to the new sound of Evida.

“This is really the first time I’ve felt confident enough to sing on our own music and after putting in so much work with this song and this sound, I’m glad that this is how we get to show people a different side of us and more of the real me.” Liam Jones

They currently create dance music containing elements of House, Hip-hop, Afropop and Trap.
